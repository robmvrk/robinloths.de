About

German (native) · English · Spanish · Portuguese · Latinum

Robin André Loths is a German-born entrepreneur and medical student based in Asunción, Paraguay. He studied Business Administration and Economics in Hohenheim and is currently enrolled in medicine at Universidad María Auxiliadora (UMAX).

He is the founder of Stuttgart Capital E.A.S. He previously co-founded and led Park Lofts E.A.S. as President, growing it to eight-figure group revenue with five residential buildings sold out during his tenure. Prior to that, Robin served as Development Advisor to Grupo Vierci Development, a Paraguayan family-office–backed real estate developer, and as a consultant at JBT Unternehmensberatung GmbH in Stuttgart.

He has been featured in Forbes Paraguay and 5DÍAS, Paraguay's leading financial media.
