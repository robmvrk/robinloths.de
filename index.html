About

German-born.
Based in Asunción.

Robin Loths is a German-born entrepreneur and medical student based in Asunción, Paraguay. He founded Stuttgart Capital E.A.S. — a real estate marketing firm presenting residential projects in Paraguay's prime market to international buyers.

He has been featured in Forbes Paraguay and 5DÍAS, Paraguay's leading financial newspaper, and recognised as a 5DÍAS Power Player among the country's top 100 financial executives. Robin holds two diplomas from the Bolsa de Valores de Asunción in Securities Markets and Financial Derivatives.

Alongside his entrepreneurial work, Robin is enrolled as a full-time medical student in Asunción.
